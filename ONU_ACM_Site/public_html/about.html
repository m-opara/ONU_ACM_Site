The Mission

We are the Local Association of Computing Machinery at Ohio Northern University (ACM@ONU). Our goal is to be the local organization for individuals who want to apply their passion for technology and do amazing things. The reason that we are an organization is to allow like minded individuals to have a place to meet others like themselves and provide an opportunity to showcase the ability of a group of people with a common goal. Our aim is to be able to teach our members concepts that they can use not only now but also in the various work environments that they might find themselves in after their academic carriers.We also know the value of unwinding and having fun, so we strive to create events that are what are members want to attend and are looking forward to.
