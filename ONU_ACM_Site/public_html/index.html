The Mission

The Local Association of Computing Machinery at Ohio Northern University (ACM@ONU) is the organization for those with a passion for all things Tech! The organization's goal is to have a place for like minded students to gather together to learn about various forms of technology, through workshops, guest speaker events, local challenges, competitions, and more. We not only attend events like this but also host events like this, and we welcome all to join, Comp Sci or otherwise. While we do encourage ACM organization membership we do not require it for the majority of our activities.

Stay in the loop

Join the Discord to stay up to date with the ACM@ONU community, and check out our social media from the join page or navigation bar above, from there you can also join our mailing list. If you would like to reach the current Council with a question or Idea, check out the Contact Us Page. We look forward to hearing from you.

The 2019 - 2020 Council

For more information on the 2019 - 2020 Council, check out the Council section of the site for a description of your student representatives, who knows, you may learn something about them you may have not known. On that page you can also find the contact information for each Council member. If you would like to reach out, they’re always happy to help members whenever they can. Interested in becoming a member of the council for the 2020-2021 academic year? Find the poll registration form and fill out the required fields at the bottom of the page. Then the registered candidates will be voted on at the end of the 2019 - 2020 academic year.

Are you in the Member Archive?

In an effort to create a digital member archive of ACM@ONU members we’ve created a small database section to store the names, photos, and descriptions of past and present members, which will someday act as an alumni yearbook. If you are interested in leaving your mark on ACM@ONU history, then add yourself to the archive section of the site.

Our Gallery

Interested to see what being apart of ACM@ONU looks like? Check out our Gallery! This Holds memories of events past and present, big and small. If you’ve taken photos at one of our events and think they should be added to our galley, use the upload form to submit them so that all our members can enjoy these amazing memories.

Come to one of our events

ACM@ONU is always striving to put on a large diverse selection of events to cater to the needs of its members. Believe it or not, coming to our events is a great way to support us! The more people we have attend, who are interested in what we are doing, the easier it is to get approval for future events and partner with sponsors. To stay up to date on what's happening and see the calendar for the year check out the Events section of the site.

Interested in joining?

ACM@ONU Welcomes all students of Ohio Northern to join! We are an organization for those with a passion for Tech, in all of its forms. For information on joining check the joining page of this site. We do not require an official ACM membership for the majority of our events.

Sponsoring our local chapter

We are so grateful for sponsorship because without it, many of our events ideas would not be possible. For a list of current or past sponsors check out the sponsor page of this site. Interested in becoming one of our sponsors? Please fill out the contact form in the sponsors section of the site.
